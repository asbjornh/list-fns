# Changelog

## 1.2.0

- Adds `has`

## 1.1.0

- Adds `byValue`
- Adds `groupByProperty`
- Adds `or`
- Adds support for returning `undefined` in `groupBy`.

## 1.0.0

- No breaking changes.
- Adds new functions:
  - `is`
  - `isBy`
  - `isOneOf`
  - `isOneOfBy`
  - `isnt`
  - `isntBy`
  - `isntOneOf`
  - `isntOneOfBy`
  - `propertyIs`
  - `propertyIsOneOf`
  - `propertyIsnt`
  - `propertyIsntOneOf`

## 0.3.0

- Adds `duplicates`, `duplicatesBy` and `duplicatesByProperty`

## 0.2.0

- Adds `countBy`
